The Story of The Week

Chapter 1

Molly had been starting out of her window for about an hour now. On her desk, laying between the copies of Nature, New Scientist, and all other journals her work had appeared in, was a well thumbled copy of On The Road. It had been Molly's favorite book since college, and the longer she spent in these four walls the more she felt she needed to be free.

She had spent the last ten years in this room, sitting under a poster with an Oscar Wilde quote proclaming that Work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do. Although many considered her pioneering work, unraveling the secrets of the llama DNA, Molly did think she had something better to do.
